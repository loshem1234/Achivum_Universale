The Chamber Awaits

You have passed the threshold and been found worthy. What this sanctum holds beyond these first three portals has not yet been revealed — not because it does not exist, but because it is not yet time for it to be shown.

The keeper is preparing what will be placed here. Your presence has been noted, your name written in the ledger of those who sought and found the gate.

What is veiled is not absent. It waits.
